Casa Blanca

Real Madrid CF is a Spanish professional football club based in Madrid. Founded in 1902 as Madrid Football Club, the club has traditionally worn a white home kit since its inception. Real Madrid is one of the most successful football clubs in the world, having won a record 14 European Cups/UEFA Champions League titles. The club has also won 35 La Liga titles, 19 Copa del Rey titles, and 12 Supercopa de España titles.

Asi gana el Madrdid

Real Madrid is one of the three founding members of La Liga that have never been relegated from the top division since its inception in 1929 (along with Athletic Bilbao and Barcelona). Being one of the most successful clubs in the world, Real Madrid has many long-standing rivalries, most notably El Clásico with Barcelona and El Derbi Madrileño with Atlético Madrid. Real Madrid's home stadium is the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium, which has a capacity of over 81,000 spectators. The club's current manager is Carlo Ancelotti. Real Madrid is one of the most popular football clubs in the world, with a large and passionate fan base. The club is also one of the most valuable football clubs in the world, with an estimated value of over €4 billion.
